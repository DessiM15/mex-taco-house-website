¡Hola Amigos!

Welcome To Mex Taco House

At Mex Taco House, we're passionate about serving an authentic Mexican dining experience that's rich in flavor and tradition. From our homemade tortillas to our freshly prepared in-house red and green salsa, every detail is crafted with care. We're proud to be known not only for our delicious tacos but also for our warm, friendly service. Our mission is simple: to bring the best, most flavorful tacos in town to our community without ever compromising on quality or authenticity.

-Mex Taco

Conveniently Located

Unforgettably Delicious

Located in the heart of Cypress, Texas, Mex Taco House serves as the go-to spot for authentic Mexican breakfast and lunch. Conveniently situated along the Northwest Freeway, we are easily accessible to nearby communities such as Jersey Village, Copperfield, and parts of Northwest Houston. Whether you're a local resident craving a satisfying meal or a commuter passing through the US-290 corridor, we're here to provide you with fresh, high-quality tacos and an unforgettable Mexican dining experience.
